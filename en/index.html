WHO
WE ARE

Multidisciplinary team formed by Philosophers, Psychologists, Communicators, Programmers and Researchers in search of the perfect combination of our knowledge for the sake of education. We try to contribute each one of our specialties to promote the quality of our students’ learning.

From the promotion of reflection in the classroom to the implementation of the newest web programming methodologies. From the differentiated treatment to the group dynamics, the entire Vía Codigo team works together to ensure the effectiveness of our curriculum.

PROGRAM
METHODOLOGY

Vía Código bases its teaching methodology on competency-based learning, a type of education that focuses on the development of generic or transversal competences (instrumental, interpersonal and systemic) and specific competences (specific to each profession). The objective of competency-based learning is to prepare the subject for development in diverse and complex contexts, integrating their knowledge and their own attitudes and values, personally and professionally. This way, we use programming as a motor for the development of both technical and human competences.

COMPREHENSIVE SELECTION

A personalized evaluation methodology covers 5 areas: Technological Affinity, Life Plan, Family Support Network, Cognitive Skills and Communication Skills.

PERSONALIZED TEACHING

We know that education begins with the bond. That is why four professionals, including psychologists and programmers, accompany the students in each class.

PSYCHOLOGICAL ACCOMPANIMENT

With personal therapy, group workshops, soft skills development, gender work, life plan development and more, our psychological team manages to intensely impact the lives of the participants.

QUALITY TRAINING

Students become front-end developers of international level. They learn Fundamentals of Computing to understand what is happening when they talk with computers. They master HTML to enhance the content of the websites and receive extra training by nationally and internationally recognized professionals.
