Mentoring Program

Each teen from Vía Código is paired with an international mentor. We have the support of developers from more than 6 countries, working at companies such as Google, Apple or Trello

The mentors help the Viacoders to discover the doors and borders that open the world of programming, and to recognize that there are people willing to not judge them and to help them grow.

The mentoring program is done by handwritten letters, and the topics go far beyond the code. Through these relationships, children learn not only to face programming, but also the labor and social challenges that they have to overcome.

Programming is an opportunity for them to redefine their masculinity, their role in society and how they want to relate to others. Creative work, even if supported by technology, is a powerful and super therapeutic vehicle to channel that power that adolescent men begin to develop at that age.
Denisse - Hiperderecho

PROGRAM

MODULE I: HTML, CSS AND SKILLS FOR LIFE

For 6 months, 4 days a week, students from Vía Código develop their development skills (HTML and CSS) while working on their protective factors from the evaluation of their own history and its reconstruction from the same process learning in the classroom.

MODULE II: PROGRAMMING PRINCIPLES, DOM, EVENTS AND LEADERSHIP

For 3 months, 4 days a week, students from Vía Código study basic programming concepts while practicing JavaScript syntax, and develop leadership exercises and teamwork.

MODULE III: TEAM WORK, BUDGETS, CLIENTS AND HOW TO GO TO PRODUCTION

During the last 3 months of the course, students from Vía Código develop real projects through which they learn skills for future work as programmers, while they build their own life story and personal goals with proper guidance.
